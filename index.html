THE PROBLEM

MEDIA BIAS

According to a study by RAND, 41% think the news is less reliable than it used to be. In an era of "fake news", consumers have to use more critical thinking to discern truth.

There's graphs showing which media outlets are skewed to the right and to the left. Some might say, "I'll just read the impartial ones" but even, within those media outlets journalists might favor one side over the other.

It's sometimes tricky to discern bias, especially when we have our own.

THE SOLUTION

A chrome extension that shows you how biased the article is and which side it favors.

HOW IT WORKS

First the extension, parses the HTML for text. Then the Google Cloud Processing platform classifies the text. We then send back a score.
